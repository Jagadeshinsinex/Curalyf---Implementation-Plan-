Detailed Clinical Implementation Strategy & Specification

Clinical Objective: Closing the "Care Gap"

The Challenge: Post-discharge care is often fragmented. Patients lose access to immediate professional guidance, leading to medication non-adherence (which accounts for ~50% of treatment failures in chronic disease) and delayed responses to critical vital sign changes.

The Curalyf Solution: A comprehensive, integrated ecosystem designed to extend the physician's reach into the patient's home. By combining intelligent automated triage with direct physician oversight, Curalyf aims to reduce readmissions and improve long-term clinical outcomes.

Technical Reliability & Compliance

Beyond clinical features, the platform is engineered for security and scalability, adhering to modern standards comparable to banking systems.

  • Data Security & ABHA Readiness

    Built on Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row-Level Security (RLS). Prepared for integration with ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) for seamless interoperability with national health records.

  • Scalable Architecture

    Designed using Next.js 16 for rapid performance on any device (Smartphone to Desktop). The "Backend-First" approach ensures that as patient volume grows, the system remains fast and reliable.

  • Future-Proof AI Integration

    The structured data collected today (Adherence + Vitals) lays the foundation for predictive analytics (Phase 5), allowing us to forecast complications before they happen.
